About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Copenhagen, and happy to be advised by Rasmus Pagh. I am interested in algorithmic differential privacy broadly, and problems in the continual observation setting in particular. I am also a member of Providentia, a part of BARC. From January through April 2025, I have the privilege of visiting Adam Smith at Boston University.
