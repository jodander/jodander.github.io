## About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Copenhagen advised by [Rasmus Pagh](https://rasmuspagh.net/). I am interested in [differential privacy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Differential_privacy) broadly, and problems in the continual observation setting in particular. I am also a member of [Providentia](https://www.rasmuspagh.net/providentia/), a part of [BARC](https://barc.ku.dk/).

Prior to starting my PhD, I received my master's degree in Engineering Physics from Lund Unversity in 2020. After graduating I had a stint as a Software Engineer at Ericsson, implementing 5G protocols in Downlink L1.

In my spare time I enjoy running, live music, and craft beer.
